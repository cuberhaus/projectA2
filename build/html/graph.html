graph.calculate_prob_connex_complex(n_complex, n_complex_and_connected, n_connected, numbers_x, numbers_y, numbers_y_complex, numbers_y_complex_and_connected, prob_q, times)¶

Appends probQ to numbers_x and appends the probability that a graph is connected given a probQ to numbers_y. Also, appends to numbers_y_complex the probability that a graph is complex given probQ. Finally, appends to numbers_y_complex_and_connected the probability that a graph is complex and connected given probQ.

Parameters

  • n_complex – Number of complex graphs

  • n_complex_and_connected – Number of complex and connected graphs

  • n_connected – Number of connected graphs

  • numbers_x – Values for the x-axis

  • numbers_y – Values for the y-axis

  • numbers_y_complex – Values for the y-axis

  • numbers_y_complex_and_connected – Values for the y-axis

  • prob_q – Probability of percolation on the graph

  • times – Times that the graph has been percolated

Returns

None, just appends to numbers_x, numbers_y, numbers_y_complex and numbers_y_complex_and_connected
